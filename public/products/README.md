## Image Specifications

- **Format:** PNG with transparent background (preferred) or JPG
- **Size:** 800x800px (square, 1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Resolution:** 72-150 DPI
- **File size:** Under 500KB (optimized for web)

## Naming Convention

Use lowercase with hyphens:
- `product-1.png`
- `product-2.png`
- etc.

## Tips

- Use clean product photography on white/transparent background
- Center the product in the frame
- Ensure good lighting and clear details
- Show product from front angle
- Keep consistent style across all images
